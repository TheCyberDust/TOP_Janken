Rules To The Game:

A classic two-person game. Whether refered to by it's standard name, Rock, Paper, Scissors, Rochambeau, or even Janken, the goal of the game is simple. Beat your opponent by picking the object that "beats" the other player's choice. Players start each round by rhythmicly saying, “rock, paper, scissors, shoot!” On “shoot,” each player holds out their fist for rock, a flat hand for paper, or their index and middle finger for scissors. Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock. See who wins each round!
